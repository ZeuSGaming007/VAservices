About Pension Services

We provide assistance with various types of pension applications and related services. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Pension Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Pension Application
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Proof of Age (Birth Certificate, School Leaving Certificate)
  • Proof of Employment (Retirement Letter, Last Pay Slip)
  • Bank Account Details (Cancelled Cheque or Bank Statement)
Pension Update
  • Existing Pension Certificate
  • Proof of Change (Updated Information)
Pension Renewal
  • Existing Pension Certificate
  • Proof of Continuity of Pension (Latest Bank Statement)
