About PM Kisan Services

We provide assistance with the PM Kisan scheme, which offers financial support to farmers. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for PM Kisan Services

Service Type Required Documents
New PM Kisan Registration
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Ownership Document (Khata or Pattadar Passbook)
  • Bank Account Details (Cancelled Cheque or Bank Statement)
  • Mobile Number
PM Kisan Update
  • Existing Registration Number
  • Updated Documents (if any)
PM Kisan Status Check
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Registration Number
