About Passport Services

We offer Passport-related services, including new applications, renewals, and updates. Below are the required documents for each service.

Required Documents for Passport Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Passport Application
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Bank Statement)
  • Proof of Date of Birth (Birth Certificate, School Certificate)
Passport Renewal
  • Current Passport
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Bank Statement)
Passport Update
  • Current Passport
  • Proof of Change (Marriage Certificate for name change, new Address Proof)
