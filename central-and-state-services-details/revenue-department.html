About Revenue Department Services

We assist with various services offered by the Revenue Department. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Revenue Department Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Land Registration
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land Ownership Document (Khata or Pattadar Passbook)
  • Proof of Identity (Voter ID, Passport)
  • Bank Account Details (Cancelled Cheque or Bank Statement)
Property Tax Payment
  • Property Identification Number
  • Previous Tax Receipt
  • Aadhaar Card
Change of Ownership
  • Existing Ownership Document
  • Sale Deed
  • Aadhaar Card
