About Senior Citizen Card Services

We assist with the application process for Senior Citizen Cards, providing benefits and discounts to individuals aged 60 and above. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Senior Citizen Card Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Senior Citizen Card Application
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Proof of Age (Birth Certificate, Pension Document)
  • Proof of Identity (Voter ID, Passport)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
Renewal of Senior Citizen Card
  • Existing Senior Citizen Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Proof of Updated Information (if applicable)
