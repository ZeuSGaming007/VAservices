About RTO Services

We assist with various services provided by the RTO, including Driving License applications, Registration Certificates, and Vehicle Extracts. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for RTO Services

Service Type Required Documents
Driving License Application
  • Aadhaar Card or other proof of identity
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Learning License (if applicable)
Registration Certificate (RC) Application
  • Proof of Vehicle Purchase (Invoice)
  • Aadhaar Card or other proof of identity
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Vehicle Insurance Document
Vehicle Extract Application
  • Vehicle Registration Number
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
