About Water and Electricity Bill Services

We assist with the payment and management of water and electricity bills. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Water and Electricity Bill Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Water Bill Registration
  • Aadhaar Card or other proof of identity
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Property Tax Receipt (if applicable)
New Electricity Bill Registration
  • Aadhaar Card or other proof of identity
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Property Tax Receipt (if applicable)
Bill Payment
  • Consumer Number
  • Payment Method (Cash, Card, Online)
Bill Dispute Resolution
  • Previous Bill Copies
  • Proof of Payment (if applicable)
  • Written Complaint (if applicable)
