About Police Department Services

We assist with various services provided by the Police Department, including job verification applications and more. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Police Department Services

Service Type Required Documents
Job Verification Application
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Resume/CV
  • Proof of Identity (Voter ID, Passport)
  • Employment Offer Letter
FIR Copy Request
  • FIR Number
  • Proof of Identity (Voter ID, Passport)
Character Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Proof of Identity (Voter ID, Passport)
  • Application Form for Character Certificate
