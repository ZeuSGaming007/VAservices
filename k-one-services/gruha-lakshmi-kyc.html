About Gruha Lakshmi KYC Services

We provide assistance with the Know Your Customer (KYC) process for the Gruha Lakshmi scheme. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Gruha Lakshmi KYC Services

Service Type Required Documents
New KYC Application
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Passbook or Bank Statement
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
KYC Update
  • Existing KYC Document
  • Proof of Updated Information (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
KYC Verification
  • Application Number (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Contact Information
