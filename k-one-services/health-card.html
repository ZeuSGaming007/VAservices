About Health Card / ABHA Card Services

We provide assistance with obtaining and updating Health Cards or ABHA Cards. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Health Card / ABHA Card Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Health Card / ABHA Card Application
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Proof of Identity (Voter ID, Passport)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
Health Card / ABHA Card Update
  • Existing Health Card / ABHA Card
  • Proof of Updated Information (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
Health Card / ABHA Card Verification
  • Application Number (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Contact Information
