About Birth Certificate Services

We provide assistance with obtaining and updating birth certificates. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Birth Certificate Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Birth Certificate Application
  • Hospital Discharge Summary
  • Parents' Aadhaar Cards
  • Proof of Identity of Parents (Voter ID, Passport)
  • Birth Notification from the hospital (if applicable)
Birth Certificate Update
  • Existing Birth Certificate
  • Proof of Updated Information (if any)
  • Parents' Aadhaar Cards
Birth Certificate Verification
  • Application Number (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card
