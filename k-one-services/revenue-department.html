About Revenue Department Services

We assist with various services provided by the Revenue Department, including land records, property assessments, and more. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Revenue Department Services

Service Type Required Documents
Land Record Application
  • Title Deed of the Property
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Survey Number Details
Property Assessment
  • Existing Property Details
  • Proof of Ownership (Title Deed)
  • Previous Assessment Notice (if applicable)
Property Mutation
  • Title Deed of the Property
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Application Form for Mutation
