About Property Tax Services

We assist with the application process for Property Tax assessments and payments. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Property Tax Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Property Tax Application
  • Title Deed of the Property
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Property Map (if applicable)
Property Tax Payment
  • Previous Tax Receipt
  • Property Identification Number (if applicable)
Property Tax Renewal
  • Previous Tax Receipt
  • Updated Property Details (if any)
