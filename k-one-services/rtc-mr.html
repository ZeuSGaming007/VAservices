About RTC and MR Services

We assist with the application process for RTC (Record of Rights, Tenancy, and Crops) and MR (Mutation Record). Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for RTC and MR Services

Service Type Required Documents
New RTC Application
  • Title Deed of the Property
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Ration Card)
  • Survey Number Details
Mutation Record Application
  • Title Deed of the Property
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Application Form for Mutation
RTC Renewal
  • Existing RTC Document
  • Proof of Updated Information (if applicable)
