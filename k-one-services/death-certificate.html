About Death Certificate Services

We provide assistance with obtaining and updating death certificates. Below are the required documents for these services.

Required Documents for Death Certificate Services

Service Type Required Documents
New Death Certificate Application
  • Death Notification from the Hospital/Doctor
  • Proof of Identity of the Deceased (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Identity of the Applicant (Aadhaar Card, Passport)
  • Address Proof of the Deceased
Death Certificate Update
  • Existing Death Certificate
  • Proof of Updated Information (if any)
  • Proof of Identity of the Applicant
Death Certificate Verification
  • Application Number (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card of the Applicant
